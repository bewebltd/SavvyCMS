Creating Dynamic Modules - Webinar: December 10, 2013, 11AM EST

With any website project, you want to focus on the domain problems you want to solve and not the mundane task of creating custom content types.

Titles, descriptions, URLs, and potentially dozens of essential data points must be captured in a custom module, making it far more complex than simply grouping fields and slapping on a name. But meeting your unique requirements shouldn't be so painful.

Join us for this webinar for a demonstration of the trusted CMS tools you can use to quickly create custom content types that can be stored and extended for future use.

During the live webinar, you'll learn how to:

  • Rapidly create modules for projects, while still providing a framework that can be customized.
  • Create content with all of its different elements, control data structure and configure the presentation in less time.
  • Define new data models and create one-to-one, one-to-many or many-to-many associations with other content types.
